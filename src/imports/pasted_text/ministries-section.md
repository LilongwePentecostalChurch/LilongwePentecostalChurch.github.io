MINISTRIES TAB BAR
Full width horizontal tab strip directly below the section header. Dark maroon background #1A0500 with thin orange top and bottom border lines. All seven ministry tabs sit in a single row. On desktop all tabs visible. On mobile tab bar scrolls horizontally with orange fade gradient on right edge hinting more tabs exist.
The tab bar automatically rotates from left to right in a continuous slow loop — each tab stays active for 4 seconds before advancing to the next. Auto rotation pauses when user hovers or clicks a tab manually. Resumes 2 seconds after user stops interacting. Loops infinitely back to first tab. A thin animated orange progress bar below the tab strip fills left to right over 4 seconds then resets on each tab change.
Each tab pill button states:
Default state: dark semi-transparent maroon background, white Montserrat Bold label, relevant icon left of label in white, no border.
Active state: orange fill #E8821A, white Montserrat Bold label, white icon, soft orange glow shadow underneath.
Hover state: orange border appears, label turns orange, icon turns orange, smooth 0.2s transition.
The seven tabs in order:
Tab 1 — SUNDAY SCHOOL — open book icon
Tab 2 — WOMEN'S MINISTRY — flower icon
Tab 3 — YOUTH MINISTRY — lightning bolt icon
Tab 4 — MEN'S MINISTRY — shield icon
Tab 5 — COUPLES MINISTRY — heart icon
Tab 6 — EVANGELISM — globe icon
Tab 7 — INTERCESSORS — hands raised icon

MINISTRY CONTENT PANEL
Directly below the tab bar. Full width. The content panel updates when each tab is clicked or when auto-rotation advances. Transition: current content fades out and scales down slightly, new content fades in and scales up — smooth 0.3s transition.
Each ministry content panel has a two column layout:
Left column — Ministry Details:
Signature script label in orange italic: the ministry name
TAN-BUSTER Bold white heading: the ministry tagline
Thin orange rule below heading.
Montserrat Regular white body text: two to three sentences describing the ministry vision and what members do.
Below body text: three or four ministry highlights in a list — each item has a small orange arrow icon on the left and Montserrat Bold white text on the right.
Below highlights: two pill buttons side by side — first button orange fill "Join This Ministry" in Montserrat Bold white, second button white outline "Learn More" in Montserrat Bold white. Both buttons have smooth orange fill on hover.
Below buttons: small Montserrat white text showing meeting day and time with a small orange clock icon on the left.
Right column — Ministry Visual:
Large atmospheric photo of the ministry in action filling the right column. Dark orange-maroon gradient overlay at 40% opacity over the photo. Photo has slightly rounded corners and a thin orange border glow. On hover photo scales subtly to 1.02x.
Floating card bottom left of the photo: dark semi-transparent maroon background, orange border, Montserrat Bold white text showing ministry leader name, Signature script orange text showing their title such as "Ministry Leader", small circular portrait photo of the leader with orange ring border.
